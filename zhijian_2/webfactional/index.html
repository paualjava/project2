One of a Kind Homewares

Lovingly handmade in London

The Colour Nation sells one of a kind homewares lovingly handmade in London for a design conscious crowd. All our products are made from beautiful West African Ankara fabrics in a riot of colour, sourced from London's markets by The Colour Nation team.


The Colour Nation was born out of a desire to throw out the rulebook in favour of unique, affordable homewares. Our first collection is called Kirike, named after a little known language from Nigeria's Niger Delta. Pieces take their name from the only existing Kirike-English dictionary, written by Levi Sika, a late Nigerian scholar and grandfather of The Colour Nation's founder.


Our dream at The Colour Nation is to bring together British makers with up-and-coming print designers from Africa and the rest of the world. One day we will make everything, from wallpaper to upholstered furniture.


Until then, check out our Pinterest page and blog to see what inspires us and sign up to our newsletter for updates on new products and collections.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What do you charge for delivery?

    Our delivery prices are based on on the size and weight of what you're buying, from £3.99 to £4.99. Follow the Newsletter for free delivery promotions from time to time.

    Do you deliver outside of the UK?

    We offer a standard delivery service to the UK. For orders outside the UK fill in a quick query and we'll do our best to accommodate your requirements.

  • Do you offer refunds?

    We offer full refunds and exchanges up to 14 days after purchase when the items are in resellable condition. Just contact us via the form below or through the shop and we'll get back to you.

    What type of cards do you accept?

    We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa Credit, Visa Debit and Mastercard. You can also pay via PayPal.

  • Do you offer commissions?

    We can make cushions to the size required by you. Contact us for more details.

    Do you offer a gift wrapping service?

    During the christmas season we will be offering a giftwrapping service.

  • How do I take care of my product?

    To care for your Colour Nation product cool wash with mild soap on a gentle cycle or by hand. Wash with with like colours and air dry. Do not leave it in direct sunlight as over a long period of time the colours could start to bleach out.
